# Swiss Birds Quiz

Swiss Birds Quiz helps people learn to recognize common Swiss birds through short offline practice sessions.

## Language

**Swiss Bird**:
A bird species commonly encountered in Switzerland.
_Avoid_: Animal, wildlife

**Practice Session**:
A short attempt to recognize and memorize Swiss birds.
_Avoid_: Exam, test

## Relationships

- A **Practice Session** asks about one or more **Swiss Birds**.

## Example dialogue

> **Dev:** "Should a **Practice Session** require internet access?"
> **Domain expert:** "No, the goal is memorizing **Swiss Birds** while offline."

## Flagged ambiguities

- "quiz" is project shorthand for **Practice Session**, not a formal graded exam.
